AU is a live video installation that allows viewers to experience the concept of a shared present between two observers separated by one Astronomical Unit (AU)    +

Aristarchus of Samos (some 2200 years ago) roughly calculated the distance from the earth to the moon, and the sun. His work unlocked a series of subsequent measurements that culminated in the what today is called the astronomical unit (AU) Because of today's knowledge of the distance between the earth and every other object in the universe, and the speed of light; we understand that when two observers are located far away from each other they are unable to experience the same present. there is no universal present. This means there is no way for two observers to experience the same present.
